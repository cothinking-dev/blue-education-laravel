* Fees & Cost Information

Blue Education's fee structure varies based on individual circumstances, service requirements, and the complexity of each case.

** Blue Education Service Fees

*** Consultation Services
- Initial consultations available (contact for details)
- Expert assessment of eligibility and options
- Personalized pathway planning
- Transparent fee structure communicated during initial consultation

*** Student Travel Advice and Consent (STAC) Charges
- Fees vary depending on the applicant's visa history and specific circumstances
- Blue Education provides transparent fee calculations based on individual cases
- Fee calculator available as a reference tool

** Government & Third-Party Costs

*** Medical Examination Costs
Required health assessments for visa applications:
- *Standard health check:* ~$240
- *Comprehensive medical assessment:* ~$380
- Type of examination depends on visa requirements

*** Visa Application Fees
- Set by the Australian Government Department of Home Affairs
- Vary by visa subclass and applicant circumstances
- Separate from Blue Education's service fees

*** Course Tuition Fees
- Paid directly to educational institutions
- Vary by institution, program, and duration
- Set by respective institutions

** Student Responsibilities

Students are responsible for arranging and covering costs for:

- *Accommodation* - Homestay, rental, or student housing fees
- *Guardianship* - Required for students under 18 (via Professional Student Care Australia or International Student Alliance)
- *OSHC* - Overseas Student Health Cover (mandatory for all student visas)
- *Living expenses* - Personal costs, transport, food
- *Tuition* - Course fees paid directly to institutions

** Transparency Commitment

All fees are clearly communicated during initial consultation. Blue Education maintains its founding principle of honesty by providing realistic cost expectations and ensuring clients understand their financial commitments before proceeding.

*Note:* Educational institution fees, visa application charges, and government-mandated costs are set by respective authorities and are separate from Blue Education's service fees.
