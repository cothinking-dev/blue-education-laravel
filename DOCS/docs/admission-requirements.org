* Admission Criteria & Requirements

Standardized requirements for entry into Australian educational programs, varying by level and institution.

** English Language Requirements

*** Accepted English Proficiency Tests

Australian institutions accept the following test results for visa and admission purposes:

| Test | Full Name |
|------|-----------|
| IELTS | International English Language Testing System (Academic or General) |
| TOEFL | Test of English as a Foreign Language |
| Cambridge CAE | Certificate in Advanced English |
| Pearson PTE | Pearson Test of English |

*** Minimum Scores by Program Level

| Program Level | IELTS Minimum | Individual Band Minimum |
|--------------|---------------|------------------------|
| Bachelor Degree | 6.5 overall | Varies by institution |
| Graduate Certificate/Diploma | 6.5 - 7.0 overall | No band below 6.0 - 6.5 |
| Master/Doctoral | 6.5 - 7.0 overall | No band below 6.0 - 6.5 |

*** Important Notes on English Requirements
- Specific institutions may have higher requirements
- Course English requirements may differ from visa English requirements
- Some institutions offer English pathway programs for students below direct entry requirements
- English test scores are typically valid for 2 years
- ELICOS courses available for students who need to improve proficiency first

** Academic Requirements

| Entry Level | Academic Requirement |
|-------------|---------------------|
| Foundation Programs | Completion of high school (Year 11 or equivalent) |
| VET/TAFE Programs | Varies by qualification level and program |
| University Entry (Bachelor) | Australian Year 12 equivalent OR VET Diploma/Advanced Diploma |
| Postgraduate Programs | Relevant Bachelor degree or equivalent |

** Additional Considerations

- *Subject prerequisites:* Some programs require specific subjects (e.g., sciences for engineering)
- *Professional programs:* Teaching, nursing, engineering may have registration requirements
- *Creative arts:* Portfolio or interview requirements may apply
- *Postgraduate:* Some programs require relevant work experience
- *Country-specific:* Requirements may vary based on country of origin

** Pathway Options for Students Below Direct Entry

1. *ELICOS* - English Language Intensive Courses (10-30 week blocks)
2. *Foundation Studies* - Pre-university bridging programs
3. *VET/TAFE Diploma* - Can pathway to university entry
4. *Packaged offers* - Combined English + academic programs
