* Student Support & Welfare Services

Blue Education provides comprehensive support services to ensure international students have a safe, comfortable, and successful experience in Australia.

** 1. Accommodation Services

*What it is*
Housing arrangement services through the Australian Homestay Network (AHN).

*Who it's for*
International students requiring accommodation in Australia.

*Key Features*
- Vetted homestay families
- Cultural immersion opportunities
- Safe and supportive living environments
- Meal options included
- Short-term and long-term arrangements

** 2. Guardianship Services

*What it is*
Legal guardianship for international students under 18 years of age, provided through Professional Student Care Australia and International Student Alliance.

*Who it's for*
Minor students (under 18) studying in Australia without parental accompaniment.

*Key Features*
- Australian legal guardian appointment
- Welfare monitoring and support
- School liaison and communication
- Emergency response coordination
- Parental reporting and updates
- Compliance with Department of Home Affairs requirements

** 3. 24/7 Emergency Hotline

*What it is*
Round-the-clock emergency support line for students in crisis situations.

*Who it's for*
All Blue Education clients, particularly students living in Australia.

*Key Features*
- Immediate response to emergencies
- Crisis management support
- Multilingual assistance
- Coordination with local services
- Peace of mind for students and families

** 4. Airport Transfers

*What it is*
Safe and reliable transportation from Australian airports to accommodation.

*Who it's for*
Newly arrived international students.

*Key Features*
- Meet and greet services
- Direct transfer to accommodation
- Assistance with luggage
- First-day orientation
- Smooth arrival experience

** 5. Document Translation Services

*What it is*
Professional translation of documents for visa, education, and migration purposes.

*Who it's for*
Clients requiring certified translations of non-English documents.

*Key Features*
- NAATI certified translations
- Academic document translation
- Legal document translation
- Fast turnaround times
- Acceptance by Australian authorities

** 6. Health Insurance (OSHC)

*What it is*
Overseas Student Health Cover arrangement and support.

*Who it's for*
All international students (mandatory requirement for student visas).

*Key Features*
- Policy selection and enrollment
- Coverage explanation
- Claims assistance
- Policy renewal management
- Compliance with visa requirements

** 7. Orientation & On-boarding

*What it is*
Comprehensive introduction to life and study in Australia.

*Who it's for*
New arrivals to Australia.

*Key Features*
- Campus orientation
- Local area familiarization
- Banking and essential services setup
- Public transport guidance
- Australian lifestyle and culture introduction
- Student support services overview

** 8. Personal Development Programs

*What it is*
Holistic development programs supporting student growth beyond academics.

*Who it's for*
International students seeking to develop personally and professionally.

*Key Features*
- Leadership development
- Cultural adaptation support
- Social skills development
- Confidence building
- Community engagement opportunities

** 9. Fees and Investment Information

*** Overview

Blue Education's fee structure varies based on individual circumstances, service requirements, and the complexity of each case.

*** Key Cost Considerations

*Student Travel Advice and Consent (STAC) Charges*
Fees vary depending on the applicant's history and specific circumstances. Blue Education provides transparent fee calculations based on individual cases.

*Medical Examination Costs*
Required health assessments for visa applications range from $240 to $380, depending on the type of examination required (standard health check vs. comprehensive medical assessment).

*Additional Service Fees*
Blue Education offers various support services for additional fees:
- 24/7 emergency hotline access
- Document translation services (NAATI certified)
- Airport transfer arrangements
- Short-tour program coordination
- Specialized consultation services

*** Student Responsibilities

Students are responsible for arranging and covering costs for:
- Accommodation fees (homestay, rental, or student housing)
- Guardianship services (for students under 18)
- Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC) - mandatory for all student visas
- Living expenses and personal costs
- Course tuition fees (paid directly to institutions)

*** Fee Calculator

Blue Education provides a fee calculator tool to help prospective students estimate their investment based on their specific situation and service needs.

*** Transparency Commitment

All fees are clearly communicated during initial consultation. Blue Education maintains its founding principle of honesty by providing realistic cost expectations and ensuring clients understand their financial commitments before proceeding.

*Note:* Educational institution fees, visa application charges, and government-mandated costs are set by respective authorities and are separate from Blue Education's service fees.

** Support Services Journey

Blue Education's support services accompany students through every stage:

1. *Pre-Arrival*: Document translation, accommodation arrangement, OSHC setup
2. *Arrival*: Airport transfers, orientation and onboarding
3. *Ongoing*: 24/7 emergency hotline, guardianship (if under 18), personal development
4. *Throughout*: Cultural adaptation support, welfare monitoring, family communication

This comprehensive support network ensures students can focus on their studies while feeling safe, supported, and integrated into Australian life.
