* Migration & Visa Services

Blue Education provides comprehensive visa and migration services to support international students and skilled professionals throughout their Australian journey.

** 1. Student Visas (Subclass 500)

*What it is*
Visa application support for international students planning to study in Australia.

*Who it's for*
International students enrolled in registered Australian courses.

*Key Features*
- Complete application preparation
- Document verification and compilation
- Genuine Temporary Entrant (GTE) statement assistance
- Financial capacity documentation
- Health and character requirement guidance

** 2. Graduate/Post-Study Work Visa (Subclass 485)

*What it is*
Temporary visa allowing international graduates to live, work, and study in Australia after completing their studies.

*Who it's for*
Recent graduates from Australian educational institutions.

*** Visa Streams

*Graduate Work Stream*
- Duration: 18 months
- For graduates with skills and qualifications on relevant skilled occupation lists
- Trade qualification or specific degree requirements

*Post-Study Work Stream*
Duration varies by qualification level:
- Bachelor degree: 2 years
- Master degree: 3 years
- Doctoral degree: 4 years
- For graduates who have completed a higher education degree

*Key Features*
- Work rights in Australia
- Opportunity to gain Australian work experience
- Pathway to skilled migration
- Family inclusion options

** 3. Skilled Migration

*What it is*
Permanent residence pathways for skilled workers through points-based migration programs.

*Who it's for*
Qualified professionals with skills in demand in Australia.

*Key Features*
- Skills assessment coordination
- Points test optimization
- Expression of Interest (EOI) preparation
- State nomination assistance
- Pathway planning for permanent residence

** 4. Employer Sponsored Migration

*What it is*
Visa programs allowing Australian employers to sponsor skilled workers for temporary or permanent residence.

*Who it's for*
Skilled workers with job offers from approved Australian employers.

*** Programs Include

*Employer Nomination Scheme (ENS)*
Permanent residence through employer sponsorship

*Temporary Skill Shortage (TSS) visa*
Temporary work visa (subclass 482)

*Key Features*
- Direct pathway to permanent residence
- Employer relationship management
- Nomination application support
- Labour market testing assistance
- Standard Business Sponsor (SBS) application support for employers (5-year sponsorship eligibility)

** 5. Business Migration

*What it is*
Visa pathways for business owners, investors, and entrepreneurs.

*Who it's for*
Business people looking to own and manage a business in Australia or make significant investments.

*Key Features*
- Business plan development support
- Investment requirement guidance
- State nomination assistance
- Compliance and reporting support

** 6. Family & Partner Visas

*What it is*
Migration pathways for family reunification and partner sponsorship.

*Who it's for*
Individuals with Australian citizen, permanent resident, or eligible New Zealand citizen family members or partners.

*** Visa Types

*Partner Visas*
For married or de facto partners

*Parent Visas*
For parents of Australian citizens or permanent residents

*Family Visas*
For other eligible family relationships

*Key Features*
- Relationship evidence compilation
- Financial sponsorship documentation
- Health and character checks
- Interview preparation
- Appeals and reviews if needed

** 7. Visitor Visas

*What it is*
Short-term visa options for tourism, family visits, or business purposes.

*Who it's for*
Individuals planning temporary visits to Australia.

*Key Features*
- Application processing
- Purpose of visit documentation
- Financial capacity evidence
- Return intention demonstration

** 8. Resident Return Visa

*What it is*
Travel facility for permanent residents to re-enter Australia after traveling overseas.

*Who it's for*
Australian permanent residents who have been or plan to be outside Australia.

*Key Features*
- Maintaining travel rights
- 5-year or 3-month travel facility options
- Substantial ties to Australia assessment
- Quick processing for eligible applicants

** Migration Pathway Summary

Blue Education supports clients through various migration stages:

1. *Study Phase*: Student Visa (Subclass 500)
2. *Post-Graduation*: Graduate Work Visa (Subclass 485)
3. *Permanent Residence Options*:
   - Skilled Migration (points-based)
   - Employer Sponsored Migration
   - Business Migration
   - Family & Partner Visas
4. *Travel Flexibility*: Resident Return Visa for established permanent residents

All visa services include comprehensive document preparation, application lodgement, and ongoing support throughout the process.
