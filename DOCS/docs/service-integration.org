* Service Integration & Additional Services

Blue Education's comprehensive service offering is designed to support clients through every stage of their Australian journey, from initial planning through to permanent settlement.

** The Blue Education Pathway

Blue Education's services work together seamlessly, creating an integrated pathway:

*** 1. Discovery & Planning
- Education counseling and pathway planning
- Initial consultations to understand goals and circumstances
- Assessment of eligibility and options
- Clear roadmap for the journey ahead

*** 2. Preparation
- English language courses (ELICOS) to meet entry requirements
- Foundation studies programs for university readiness
- Student visa applications (Subclass 500)
- Pre-departure orientation and support

*** 3. Study
- Primary and secondary education placement
- Vocational education and training (VET/TAFE)
- University degrees (Bachelor, Master, Doctoral)
- Ongoing student support and welfare services
- Accommodation and guardianship arrangements
- 24/7 emergency support

*** 4. Transition
- Graduate/Post-study work visa (Subclass 485)
- Job readiness programs
- Career counseling and pathway planning
- Resume and interview preparation
- Australian workplace culture training

*** 5. Career & Settlement
- Employment placement and employer connections
- Work visa assistance
- Skilled migration pathways
- Employer sponsored migration
- Permanent residence applications
