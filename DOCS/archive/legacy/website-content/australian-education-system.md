# Australian Education System

## Study Options in Australia

Australia offers a wide range of study options for international students, with more than 1,000 institutions and over 20,000 programmes available to choose from across the entire country.

## Primary & Secondary Education

Primary and secondary education lasts for 13 years in Australia, from preparatory to senior secondary. Schooling is compulsory until at least the age of 16.

## Higher Education

Degrees and Higher Education are classified under AQF Level 7-10.

Seven of the top 100 universities in the world are in Australia, demonstrating the high quality of Australian tertiary education.

## Quality and Recognition

Graduates from Australia are highly sought because of the impressive international reputation of the Australian education system, which is carefully regulated by the Australian government to maintain high standards.

## Rising Rankings

Australia's universities are rising in world rankings, further cementing the country's position as a leading destination for international education.
