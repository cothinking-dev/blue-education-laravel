# Fees and Charges

## STAC (Student Travel Advice and Consent) Charges

STAC is charged based on the history of each applicant's visa. Blue Education offers a fee calculator as a reference tool to help estimate these costs.

## Medical Examination Costs

Medical examinations required by the Department of Home Affairs cost approximately:
- **$240 - $380** (depending on the type of medical examination required)

## Additional Support Services

Blue Education offers the following additional support services for extra fees:

### Emergency Support
- 24/7 emergency help and hotline care

### Documentation Services
- Document translation services

### Transportation
- Airport transfers and transport arrangements

### Educational Programs
- Short-tour programs

## Student Responsibilities

For students in primary and secondary education, the student is responsible for any fees and costs associated with:
- Accommodation arrangements
- Guardianship services
- Welfare arrangements

## Fee Information

For detailed fee information and personalized quotes based on your specific circumstances, please contact Blue Education directly or use their fee calculator tool.
