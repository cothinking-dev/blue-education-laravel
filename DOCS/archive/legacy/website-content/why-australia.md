# Why Australia

## Overview

Australia is an exceptional destination for international students, offering world-class education, diverse opportunities, and an outstanding quality of life.

## Institutional Options

There are over 1,100 institutions found across the entire country, providing students with extensive choices for their educational journey.

## Affordable Cost of Living

The cost of living in Australia is great, especially when compared to the UK and the USA, making it an attractive and affordable option for international students.

## Top 5 Reasons to Choose Australia

### 1. Global Recognition

Graduates from Australia are highly sought after because of the impressive international reputation of the Australian education system. The system is carefully regulated by the Australian government to maintain high standards, ensuring that qualifications are recognized and valued worldwide.

### 2. Variety of Options

Institutions in Australia offer a wide variety of courses and degrees, so international students can easily find the school and field that are right for them. Depending on their interest, students can choose between:
- Universities
- Vocational education (TAFE and private providers)
- English language training

### 3. Technology & Innovation

Australia is at the forefront of new technology and innovations, providing students with access to cutting-edge research, facilities, and learning environments that prepare them for future careers.

### 4. Work Opportunities

While the UK closes doors for doctors and the US focuses more on IT professionals, Australia offers more job facilities in multiple disciplines.

#### Post Study Work Visa (Subclass 485)

The Subclass 485 Post Study Work Visa allows international graduates to:
- Remain in Australia for 2 to 4 years after completing their studies
- Gain valuable Australian work experience
- Explore career opportunities across various industries
- Build professional networks

This visa significantly enhances employment prospects and can serve as a pathway to permanent residency.

### 5. Quality of Life

Australia offers an exceptional quality of life with:
- Natural beauty and diverse landscapes
- Safe and welcoming communities
- Outdoor lifestyle and recreational activities
- Multicultural society
- High living standards
- Excellent healthcare system
