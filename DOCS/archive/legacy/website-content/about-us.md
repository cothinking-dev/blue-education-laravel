# About Us

## Company History

In 1998, a group of professionals got together and saw the need to provide independent, honest and reliable advice to international students who wanted to live and study in Australia. Blue Education was founded based on that trust and desire to help international students navigate their Australian education journey.

## Established Since the Late 90s

Blue Education has been established since the late 90s, specialising in education guidance, counselling and international student placement in Australia. The company has been operating for over 20 years, building a strong reputation in the international education sector.

## Mission

Their mission is to be the world's leading and trusted source of advice for education, career and migration to Australia.

## Industry Recognition

Blue Education is boutique and recognised in the industry through their years of well-established relationships with:
- Universities
- TAFE institutions
- Government departments
- Other stakeholders in Australian education

## Core Values

Blue Education is committed to:
- Providing independent, honest and reliable advice
- Setting goals and planning journeys together with clients
- Managing risk and assisting clients to achieve desired outcomes
- Always keeping clients' interests at heart
- Promoting the Australian lifestyle and quality education to students from around the globe

## Service Evolution

Today, their versatile and multi-lingual team has grown to provide comprehensive services across:
- Education guidance and counselling
- Student placement
- Migration advice
- Career counselling and support

## Track Record

Blue Education has:
- Assisted thousands of clients from 40 different countries
- Operated continuously for over 20 years
- Expanded services across education, migration, and career sectors
- Built partnerships across all major Australian cities

## Team Expertise

They are a team of education, career and migration specialists, with team members who are:
- Part of the Migration Alliance
- Members of the Migration Institute of Australia
- Affiliated with the Australian Bar Association
- Qualified Education Agent Counsellor (QEAC) certified

## Official Recognition

Blue Education has received official recognition by the School Curriculum and Standards Authority (SCSA), under the WA Department of Education, allowing them to:
- Provide direct support to overseas schools
- Promote the Western Australia curriculum programme internationally
- Assist students seeking to study in Australia
- Facilitate WACE program completion for international students

## Global Experience

With over 20 years of experience across 140 countries, Blue Education brings extensive international education expertise to every client engagement.
