# Blue International Team

## Overview

Blue Education's international team consists of multilingual professionals from various industries including education, multi-national corporations and professional firms. The team's skills are multifaceted, and most team members are multilingual, providing assistance to international students and potential migrants from all over Asia, Europe, America, South America, Africa and the Middle East.

## Experience

Blue Education comes with over 20 years of experience with a team of multilingual professionals dedicated to helping with needs to study, work and migrate to Australia.
