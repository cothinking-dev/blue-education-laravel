# SCSA Associate

## About SCSA

The School Curriculum and Standards Authority (SCSA) is responsible for Kindergarten to Year 12 curriculum, assessment, standards and reporting for all Australian schools.

## Blue Education's SCSA Accreditation

Blue Education has received official recognition by the School Curriculum and Standards Authority (SCSA), under the WA Department of Education. This accreditation allows Blue Education to provide direct support to overseas schools and students seeking to study in Australia.

## Appointment and Authority

Blue Education has been appointed by the statutory authority to promote the Western Australia curriculum programme internationally. With over 20 years of experience across 140 countries, Blue Education is well-positioned to support international education initiatives.

## Western Australian Certificate of Education (WACE)

Through their SCSA accreditation, students overseas can now complete the Western Australian Certificate of Education (WACE) program without needing to relocate immediately to Australia. This provides international students with the opportunity to:
- Access Australian curriculum standards
- Earn recognized Australian qualifications
- Prepare for eventual study in Australia
- Meet Australian education requirements from their home country

## Scope of Services

Blue Education's SCSA-accredited services include:

### For Schools
- Assisting schools overseas in understanding the WA curriculum programme
- Supporting application of the WA curriculum programme
- Facilitating the onboarding and launching of the curriculum
- Providing ongoing support and guidance

### For Students
- Direct support for students seeking to study in Australia
- Pathway planning using the WACE program
- Guidance on curriculum requirements
- Preparation for Australian education standards

## Experience and Reach

With over 20 years of experience operating across 140 countries, Blue Education brings extensive expertise in international education and curriculum implementation to its SCSA partnership.
