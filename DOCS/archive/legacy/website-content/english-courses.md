# English Courses

## Overview

Blue Education offers comprehensive English language courses in Australia to help international students develop their language skills and prepare for further education or professional opportunities.

## Course Structure

Courses are offered at private English colleges and University English colleges and may be recognised by further education institutes. Classes are usually offered in block periods:
- 10 weeks
- 20 weeks
- 30 weeks

## Course Focus

The courses focus on:
- Communication skills
- Understanding English language rules
- Sentence structure
- Vocabulary development
- Grammar mastery

## Specialized English Courses

The program includes specialized teaching qualifications:
- **TESOL** - Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages
- **CELTA** - Certificate in Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages
- **EfTC** - English for Teaching Children

## Support Services

Blue Education's education counsellors provide comprehensive services including:
- Assessing student future aspirations
- Recommendations of pathways, courses and institutes in Australia
- Assistance to gain admission
- Support through the entire school and visa application process

## Location and Partners

Blue Education's headquarters is in Perth, and they have partners in all major cities in and around Australia.
