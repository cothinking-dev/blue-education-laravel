# Blue Education - Homepage

## About Blue Education

Blue Education has been established since the late 90s, specialising in education guidance, counselling and international student placement in Australia. In 1998, a group of professionals got together and saw the need to provide independent, honest and reliable advice to international students who wanted to live and study in Australia, and Blue Education was founded based on that trust and desire.

## Company Overview

Blue Education has been around for the last 20 years and is boutique and recognised in the industry through their years of well-established relationships with universities, TAFE and other stakeholders. Blue Education strives to promote the Australian lifestyle and quality education to students from around the globe.

They have assisted thousands of clients from 40 different countries in the past 20 years.

## Mission

Their mission is to be the world's leading and trusted source of advice for education, career and migration to Australia.

## Services Overview

Blue Education provides comprehensive support for international students, including:

### Education Services

Education guidance, counseling, and international student placement in Australia, providing services that include:
- Course selection
- Student visa applications
- Accommodation arrangements
- Assistance from primary school to university studies

Their education counsellors services include assessing of student future aspiration and recommendations of pathways, courses and institute in Australia. They help gain admission and assist through the entire school and visa application process.

### Migration Services

They offer migration services such as:
- Skilled migration
- Family visas
- Catering to both international students and employers

### Career Support

Blue Education assists international student graduates in:
- Connecting with potential employers
- Obtaining work visas
- Career counseling
- Job readiness programs

## Core Values

Blue Education provides honest and genuine advice to clients for studies, work and migration options. The company emphasizes that they help set goals and plan journeys together with clients, managing risk and assisting to achieve desired outcomes. They always keep clients' interests at heart.

## Team

They are a team of education, career and migration specialists, with team members who are part of the Migration Alliance, Migration Institute of Australia, Australian Bar Association and Qualified Education Agent Counsellor (QEAC) certified.

Today, their versatile and multi-lingual team has grown to provide migration and career advice.

## Location and Support

Their headquarters is in Perth, Western Australia, and they have partners in all major cities in and around Australia. They offer a range of support services, including:
- Orientation
- On-boarding sessions
- Accommodation
- Home-stay
- Farm-stay
- Transport
- Health insurance
- 24/7 hotline care
