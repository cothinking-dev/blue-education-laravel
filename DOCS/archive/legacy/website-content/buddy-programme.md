# Buddy Programme

## Overview

The Buddy Programme is an amazing and highly engaging fourteen-day educational journey designed to develop students' language, social and communication skills through immersive Australian school and cultural experiences.

## Programme Duration

The programme runs for **fourteen days**, during which students:
- Attend school with their Australian Buddies
- Live with homestay families
- Participate in cultural and educational activities

## Programme Structure

### Morning Sessions - English Lessons

The programme offers structured English lessons and discussion topics surrounding historical, cultural and geographical themes of Australia.

**English Class Themes:**
- Australian population
- Geographical settings
- Cultural traditions and customs
- Wildlife and natural environment
- Interesting places and landmarks
- Popular activities
- Australian way of life

### Afternoon Sessions - School Integration

Integration occurs in the afternoon when students get to join in the routine of the high school students.

**Subjects Covered:**
All subjects are taught in English, including:
- Science
- Mathematics
- Other core subjects
- Foreign language classes (exception to English-only instruction)

## Accommodation - Homestay Families

Students are placed with carefully selected homestay families who provide a warm and safe environment to care for their needs.

### Homestay Support Includes:
- All meals during the programme
- Safe and comfortable accommodation
- Emotional support
- Daily living assistance
- Cultural immersion opportunities

## Experiential Learning

### Local Field Trips

Local field trips are integrated into the classroom studies to:
- Elevate interest in the lessons taught
- Provide hands-on learning experiences
- Showcase Australian geography and culture
- Reinforce classroom concepts

### Voluntary Work Opportunities

Special arrangements can be made with local councils for students to participate in voluntary work. This opportunity:
- Looks great on student CVs
- Enhances scholarship applications
- Strengthens university entry programme applications
- Develops civic responsibility and community engagement
- Provides practical work experience

## Partnership

The programme works with:
- Anglican Schools
- Established homestay families in the community
- Local councils for voluntary work placements

## Programme Benefits

Students develop:
- Enhanced English language skills
- Cross-cultural communication abilities
- Social skills and confidence
- Understanding of Australian education system
- International experience for academic applications
- Personal growth and independence
