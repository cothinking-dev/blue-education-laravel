# Primary & Secondary Education

## Overview

Primary and secondary education lasts for 13 years in Australia, from preparatory to senior secondary. Schooling is compulsory until at least the age of 16.

## Services for International Students

Blue Education helps international students with primary and secondary school placements in Australia.

## Accommodation and Welfare Requirements

The Department of Immigration & Border Protection (DIBP) requires that students under the age of 18 have appropriate accommodation and welfare arrangements whilst studying in Australia.

Blue Education assists in arranging:
- **Accommodation** - Through the Australian Homestay Network (AHN)
- **Guardianship arrangements** - Through either:
  - Professional Student Care Australia
  - International Student Alliance Student Care & Welfare Services

## Comprehensive Support

Blue Education helps students:
- Gain admission to primary and secondary schools
- Navigate the entire school application process
- Complete visa application procedures
- Arrange suitable accommodation and guardianship

## Experience and Track Record

Blue Education has assisted thousands of clients from 40 different countries in the past 20 years, helping students from primary school through to university studies.

## Student Responsibilities

The student is responsible for any fees and costs associated with accommodation and guardianship arrangements.
