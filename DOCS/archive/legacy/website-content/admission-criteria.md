# Admission Criteria

## Overview

Candidates applying to study in Australia are required to meet specific English language criteria and academic requirements set by educational institutions.

## English Language Requirements

Candidates are required to meet the English Language criteria set by the school.

### Accepted English Language Tests

The following test results are accepted by the Australian Government Department of Immigration & Border Protection (DIBP) for visa purposes:
- **IELTS** (Academic or General)
- **TOEFL**
- **Cambridge (CAE)**
- **Pearson (PTE)**

## English Requirements by Program Level

### Graduate Certificate and Graduate Diploma

**Minimum English Requirements:**
- IELTS Academic: 6.5 - 7.0
- No individual band below 6.0 or 6.5

### Bachelor's Degrees

**Minimum English Requirements:**
- IELTS Academic: 6.5

**Academic Requirements:**
- Year 12 or its overseas equivalent, OR
- A VET Diploma or Advanced Diploma

## Important Considerations

### Institution-Specific Requirements

Institutions set their own English language requirements for course entry. The English proficiency score needed for your course may be different from the score needed to secure your visa.

### Country-Specific Requirements

Requirements may vary by:
- Country of origin
- Specific program of study
- Institution choice

### Final Decision

The Admissions office of respective institutions provides the final decision on acceptance of your application.
