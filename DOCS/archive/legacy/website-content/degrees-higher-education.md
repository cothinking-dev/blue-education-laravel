# Degrees & Higher Education

## Overview

Australian Universities and Private institutions offer a wide range of courses leading to three main types of qualifications:
- Bachelor (Single or Double)
- Master
- Doctoral Degrees

## Classification

Degrees and Higher Education are classified under AQF Level 7-10.

## World-Class Universities

Seven of the top 100 universities in the world are in Australia, demonstrating the exceptional quality of Australian higher education.

## Industry Recognition
