** Post-Study Work Visa (Subclass 485)
:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: Two-column — text left, table right
:LAYOUT: 55/45 split
:END:

*** Body

The Subclass 485 lets you live, work, and study in Australia after completing your qualification. It's your bridge from study to career — and to potential permanent residence.

There are two streams:

*** Graduate Work Stream

| Detail      | Info                                                                  |
|-------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Duration    | 18 months                                                             |
| Eligibility | Skills and qualifications on a relevant [[/services/migration/permanent-residence][Skilled Occupation List]]        |

*** Post-Study Work Stream

| Qualification   | Visa Duration |
|-----------------+---------------|
| Bachelor Degree | 2 years       |
| Master Degree   | 3 years       |
| Doctoral Degree | 4 years       |

Both streams include work rights in Australia, opportunity to gain Australian work experience, [[/services/migration/permanent-residence][pathway to skilled migration]], and family inclusion options.

** Employer Sponsored Visas
:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: BentoBox — 2 cards
:LAYOUT: Side by side, equal height
:END:

*** Temporary Skill Shortage (TSS) — Subclass 482

For skilled workers with a job offer from an approved Australian employer. Allows work in Australia for up to 4 years and can lead to permanent residence.

*** Employer Nomination Scheme (ENS)

Direct pathway to [[/services/migration/permanent-residence][permanent residence]] through employer sponsorship. Your employer nominates you for a permanent position. We manage the nomination application, labour market testing, and compliance.
