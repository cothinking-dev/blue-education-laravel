** The Migration Pathway
:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: Timeline — horizontal, 4 stages
:LAYOUT: Connected steps with icons, left to right on desktop, vertical on mobile
:END:

*** Study

Student Visa (Subclass 500). Enrol in a CRICOS-registered course, study full-time, and work up to 48 hours per fortnight.

/[NOTE: Verify current DHA student visa work hour limit before publishing — this figure (48hr/fortnight) reflects the cap reinstated in 2023, but policy may have been updated since. Check current DHA guidelines.]/

*** Graduate

Graduate Work Visa (Subclass 485). Remain in Australia for 18 months to 4 years after graduation — depending on your qualification level and visa stream.

*** Work

Employer Sponsorship (Subclass 482) or Skilled Migration. Your Australian work experience becomes the foundation for the next step. [[/services/career][Career services →]]

*** Settle

Permanent Residence. Points-based, employer-sponsored, business, or family pathways — each with different requirements and timelines.

/Your path may skip steps or combine them. We plan around your specific situation./
