*** Education

**** How do I choose the right course?

We assess your background, goals, and budget and recommend matching programs from 1,100+ institutions and 20,000+ programs.

**** What English level do I need?

| Program Level            | IELTS Minimum |
|--------------------------+---------------|
| Foundation               | 5.0 – 5.5    |
| VET/TAFE                 | 5.5 – 6.0    |
| Bachelor                 | 6.5           |
| Graduate Cert/Diploma    | 6.5 – 7.0    |
| Master/Doctoral          | 6.5 – 7.0    |

Postgraduate programs: no individual band below 6.0–6.5. Also accepted: TOEFL, Cambridge CAE, Pearson PTE. Scores valid for 2 years. If you're not at the required level, [[/services/education/english-foundation][ELICOS courses]] (10–30 weeks) can bridge the gap.

**** Can I change courses after I start?

Usually yes, but it may affect your visa conditions. Talk to us before making changes.

*** Migration & Visas

**** How long does a student visa take?

4–8 weeks typical. We review applications for completeness before submission to avoid delays.

**** Can I work while studying?

Yes. [[/services/migration/student-visas][Student visa]] holders can work up to 48 hours per fortnight during term and unlimited hours during breaks.

**** What happens after I graduate?

You may be eligible for a [[/services/migration/graduate-work-visas][Graduate/Post-Study Work Visa (Subclass 485)]]. Two streams:

*Graduate Work Stream:* 18 months — for graduates with skills on the skilled occupation list.

*Post-Study Work Stream:*

| Qualification | Duration |
|---------------+----------|
| Bachelor      | 2 years  |
| Master        | 3 years  |
| Doctoral      | 4 years  |

Both streams provide full work rights and a pathway to permanent residency.

*** Career

**** Do you guarantee job placement?

No. We provide career counseling, job readiness training, employer introductions, and internship placements. These significantly improve employment prospects but do not guarantee placement.

**** When should I start career planning?

Before choosing your course. Career goals should drive education decisions, not the other way around.

*** Student Support

**** What if there's an emergency?

24/7 emergency hotline. Multilingual support. All Blue Education clients covered.

**** Do you provide accommodation?

Yes. Through the Australian Homestay Network — vetted families, meals included. Guardianship arranged for under-18 students through Professional Student Care Australia or International Student Alliance. [[/services/student-support][Student support services →]]
