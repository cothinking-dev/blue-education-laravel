*** Skilled Migration
:PROPERTIES:
:TAG: Subclass 189 · 190 · 491
:END:

For qualified professionals with skills in demand in Australia. The points-based system assesses age, English proficiency, qualifications, and work experience. We handle [[/resources/admission-requirements][skills assessment]] coordination, EOI preparation, and state nomination applications.

/If your points are too low, we tell you — and show you exactly what to do about it./

*Best for:* Professionals with [[/services/migration/graduate-work-visas][Australian qualifications and work experience]] who meet or are approaching the points threshold.

*** Employer Sponsored Migration
:PROPERTIES:
:TAG: Subclass 186 · 482
:END:

Your employer nominates you for permanent or temporary residence. We manage the nomination application, labour market testing requirements, and sponsorship compliance throughout.

*Best for:* Workers with a confirmed job offer from an approved Australian employer who cannot find a suitably skilled local candidate.

*** Business Migration

For business owners, investors, and entrepreneurs planning to establish or invest in Australia. We support business plan development, investment requirement guidance, and state nomination applications.

*Best for:* Experienced business operators with significant capital or a demonstrated track record in business ownership or management.

*** Family & Partner Visas

Partner, parent, and family reunification pathways for those with Australian citizen or permanent resident family members. We compile relationship evidence, manage financial sponsorship documentation, and coordinate health and character requirements — including representation if the application requires a review.

*Best for:* Anyone with an eligible Australian citizen, permanent resident, or eligible NZ citizen family member or partner.

** How It Works
:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: Timeline — horizontal, 3 steps
:LAYOUT: Connected steps with icons
:END:

*** 1. Skills Assessment

Your occupation must be assessed by the relevant Australian authority before you can submit an Expression of Interest. We identify the appropriate assessing body for your field and coordinate the application.

*** 2. Expression of Interest (EOI)

Once assessed, you submit an EOI through SkillSelect. Invitations are issued in rounds — your points score determines when and whether you receive one. We optimise your points profile and advise on state nomination options that can increase your total.

*** 3. Application

Invitations are valid for 60 days. Once received, we prepare and lodge your visa application — documentation, health and character requirements, and compliance checks handled in full.
