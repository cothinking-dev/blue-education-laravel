** For Students
:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: BentoBox — 3 cards
:LAYOUT: 3-column grid
:END:

*** Complete WACE Overseas

Earn the Western Australian Certificate of Education from your home country — the same qualification Australian Year 12 students earn.

*** University-Ready

WACE is accepted for [[/resources/admission-requirements][direct entry]] to [[/services/education/degrees][Western Australian universities]]. Students who complete the programme overseas are assessed on the same criteria as domestic Year 12 graduates.

*** Seamless Transition

If you later choose to study in Australia, your WACE preparation means you're already aligned with Australian academic standards and teaching methods.

*** Premier's WACE Bursary

Students who complete the WACE programme overseas and enrol in a participating WA university may be eligible for the Premier's WACE Bursary — AUD $20,000, awarded to 50 applicants per year. Participating universities: UWA, Curtin, Murdoch, ECU, and Notre Dame.
