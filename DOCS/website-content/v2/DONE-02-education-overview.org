** How We Help
:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: Two-column — text left, image right
:LAYOUT: 55/45 split
:IMAGE: Counselor with student in meeting
:END:

Choosing where and what to study in Australia isn't just an education decision — it's a career decision, a migration decision, and a life decision.

Your advisor maps out institutions and programs against two questions: what career do you want, and what does your visa pathway look like? The course recommendation comes after that — not before.

Once you've decided, we take care of the rest — application, enrolment, and student visa included.

** The Placement Process
:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: Timeline — vertical, 5 steps
:LAYOUT: Numbered steps, icon + heading + short text
:END:

*** 1. Discovery Session

We assess your background, goals, and eligibility. One conversation to understand where you're starting from and where you want to end up.

*** 2. Pathway Recommendation

You receive a shortlist of institutions and programs matched to your career and migration goals — with a clear explanation of why each one fits.

*** 3. Application & Enrolment

We prepare your documents, lodge applications, and secure your offer and enrolment confirmation.

*** 4. Visa & Pre-Departure

Student visa, accommodation, health insurance, arrival orientation. All coordinated in one place.

*** 5. Ongoing Support

Academic guidance, welfare monitoring, and career planning for as long as you're studying.

|-----------------+-----------+--------------+----------------------|

Think of entry requirements as a starting point, not a ceiling. There's almost always more than one way in.

/VET post-study work rights (Graduate Work Stream, Subclass 485, 18 months) are available to graduates whose qualification and occupation appear on a skilled occupation list. Eligibility depends on course and occupation — your advisor will confirm during assessment./
