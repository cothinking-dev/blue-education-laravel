** Support Services
:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: BentoBox — 6 cards with icons
:LAYOUT: 3x2 grid on desktop, stacked on mobile
:END:

*** Accommodation
:PROPERTIES:
:ICON: home
:END:

Vetted [[/programs/buddy-programme][homestay families]] through the Australian Homestay Network. Safe, supportive living environment. Meals included. Short-term and long-term arrangements available.

*** Guardianship (Under 18)
:PROPERTIES:
:ICON: shield
:END:

Legal guardianship arranged through Professional Student Care Australia or International Student Alliance. Welfare monitoring, school liaison, parental reporting. Required by the Department of Home Affairs for unaccompanied minor students.

*** 24/7 Emergency Hotline
:PROPERTIES:
:ICON: phone
:END:

Round-the-clock crisis support. Multilingual assistance. Coordination with local emergency services. Available to all Blue Education clients.

*** Airport Transfers
:PROPERTIES:
:ICON: plane
:END:

Meet and greet at the airport. Direct transfer to accommodation. Luggage assistance included.

*** Health Insurance (OSHC)
:PROPERTIES:
:ICON: heart
:END:

Overseas Student Health Cover — mandatory for all [[/services/migration/student-visas][student visas]]. We arrange cover through our partner providers — OSHC Australia, Annalink, and Konpare — and handle policy selection, enrolment, claims assistance, and renewal.

*** Document Translation
:PROPERTIES:
:ICON: file-text
:END:

NAATI-certified translations for academic, legal, and visa documents. Accepted by all Australian authorities. Fast turnaround.
