** What is VET/TAFE?
:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: Two-column — text left, image right
:LAYOUT: 55/45 split
:IMAGE: TAFE campus, students in workshop
:END:

Vocational Education and Training (VET) is delivered through Technical and Further Education (TAFE) institutions and Registered Training Organisations (RTOs) across Australia.

VET qualifications emphasise practical, hands-on skills. You learn in workshops, labs, and real workplace settings. Graduates enter the workforce job-ready, with qualifications employers trust.

** Is VET right for you?
:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: 3-column card grid
:LAYOUT: Desktop: 3 equal-width cards, icon at top of each card, heading + short paragraph per card. Mobile: stack vertically.
:END:

*** You want to work in a trade or specialist role

Not every career requires a degree. VET qualifications are built around the skills employers actually hire for — and most can be completed in under two years.

*** You're changing careers

VET is well-suited to career changers who need industry-recognised credentials in a new field without starting from scratch academically. [[/services/career][Career services →]] can help you identify the right pathway.

*** You have migration goals

Many VET occupations sit on Australia's skilled occupation lists. The right qualification can be the most direct route to a skilled migration pathway.

** Why VET?
:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: 3-column feature list
:LAYOUT: Desktop: 3 equal columns. Each item: icon left-aligned inline with heading (not above), bold heading, short paragraph below. Open layout — no card backgrounds, no box borders, no shadow. Subtle left accent rule on each item. Mobile: stack vertically.

*** Industry-aligned training

Training happens in workshops, labs, and workplaces. What you learn maps directly to what employers expect on day one.

*** Credit towards a degree

Diploma and Advanced Diploma holders can apply for credit recognition at Australian universities — reducing the time required to complete a bachelor degree.

*** Skilled migration pathways

Many VET occupations appear on Australia's skilled occupation lists. For students with migration goals, the right qualification can open that door directly. Eligible graduates may also qualify for the Graduate Work Stream visa (18 months).
