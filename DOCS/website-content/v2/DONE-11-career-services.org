** The Career Pathway
:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: Timeline — horizontal, 6 stages
:LAYOUT: Connected steps with icons
:END:

*** 1. Assess & Plan

One-on-one session to map your skills against your goals. We identify gaps, outline realistic pathways, and review your resume before you start applying.

*** 2. Prepare

Australian workplaces operate differently to most. This step covers interview techniques, professional networking, and LinkedIn optimisation — plus the unwritten expectations that affect how you're perceived from day one.

*** 3. Build Experience

Theory only goes so far. Industry-specific internships and work placements give you the Australian work history that makes your resume credible to local employers.

*** 4. Connect with Employers

Direct introductions to employers we work with — not job boards, not cold applications. Recruitment support guided by advisors who know your field and your goals.

*** 5. Secure Your Visa

Your career plan and your visa must align. We assess your [[/services/migration/graduate-work-visas][work visa]] eligibility, prepare your application, and coordinate employer sponsorship where required.

*** 6. Advance Your Career

For business graduates and experienced professionals, the [[/programmes/executive-internship][Executive Internship Programme]] offers senior-level placements with executive mentorship. Limited places, twice a year.
