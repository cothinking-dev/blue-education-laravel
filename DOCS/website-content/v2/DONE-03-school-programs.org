** The Australian School System
:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: Two-column — infographic left, text right
:LAYOUT: 45/55 split. Left: visual diagram of 13-year school system
:END:

Australia's school system spans 13 years:

| Stage       | Years           | Ages   | What students focus on                                        |
|-------------+-----------------+--------+---------------------------------------------------------------|
| Preparatory | Foundation      | ~5     | Early learning, social development                            |
| Primary     | 1–6 (or 1–7)   | 6–12   | Core subjects across all learning areas                       |
| Lower Sec.  | 7–10 (or 8–10) | 12–16  | Broader learning areas with growing elective choices          |
| Upper Sec.  | 11–12           | 16–18  | Subjects chosen to prepare for further education or career    |

- Compulsory until age 16 in most states and territories
- Public and private schools available — we match your child to the right fit
- Blue Education is an SCSA Associate — officially named by SCSA on their international schools website as an appointed agency to promote the WA curriculum internationally. [[/programs/scsa-associate][Learn about SCSA →]]

*** Before Arrival

**** School Selection & Placement

We match your child to the right school based on location, curriculum, fees, learning profile, and long-term goals. Public and private options across Australia — we explain the differences and recommend what fits.

**** Student Visa (Subclass 500)

Full visa application management for minors — documentation, health checks, character requirements, lodgement. We handle the process end to end.

[[/services/migration/student-visas][Learn about student visas →]]

**** Accommodation

Homestay placement through the Australian Homestay Network. Vetted families, meals included, safe living environment — all arranged before your child arrives.

[[/services/student-support][Full accommodation details →]]

*** Once They're There

**** Guardianship

Legal guardianship arranged through Professional Student Care Australia or International Student Alliance. Required by the Department of Home Affairs for unaccompanied students under 18. Your child has a named responsible adult in Australia at all times.

[[/services/student-support][Full guardianship details →]]

**** Welfare Monitoring

Regular welfare checks, school liaison, and parental reporting — so you always know how your child is settling in, not just at enrolment time.

[[/services/student-support][Full welfare support details →]]

**** Cultural Adaptation

The first weeks in a new country can be disorienting for young students. We provide ongoing integration support: helping your child navigate Australian classroom culture, social norms, and daily routines. For a more structured introduction, our Buddy Programme places students alongside Australian peers for 14 days before full enrolment begins.

[[/programs/buddy-programme][Learn about the Buddy Programme →]]

**** School Transfer & Change of Programme

Already enrolled but need to change schools or programmes? We manage the full transfer — notifying the institution, updating enrolment records, and advising on any visa implications. Changes to enrolment must be reported to the Department of Home Affairs; we handle that process on your behalf.
