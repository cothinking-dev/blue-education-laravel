** English Language Requirements
:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: Table with intro text
:LAYOUT: Clean reference table, full-width
:END:

*Accepted tests:* IELTS, TOEFL, Cambridge CAE, Pearson PTE. Scores valid for 2 years.

*** Minimum IELTS Scores

| Program                      | Overall | Per-band minimum   |
|------------------------------+---------+--------------------|
| Foundation                   | 5.0–5.5 | Varies             |
| VET/TAFE                     | 5.5–6.0 | Varies             |
| Bachelor                     | 6.5     | Varies by institution |
| Graduate Cert/Diploma        | 6.5–7.0 | Minimum 6.0 in each band |
| Master/Doctoral              | 6.5–7.0 | Minimum 6.0 in each band |

Institutions may set higher thresholds. Course requirements may differ from visa requirements.

| [[/services/education/degrees][Postgraduate]]         | Relevant Bachelor degree (+ work experience for some)|

| Creative Arts        | Portfolio or interview may apply                     |

/Note for professional fields: Teaching, nursing, and engineering graduates require registration with the relevant Australian authority — Teaching Council, AHPRA (nursing and allied health), or Engineers Australia — in addition to academic admission. We advise on these requirements as part of your pathway assessment./
