:SECTION: Timeline — vertical, 5 steps
:LAYOUT: Numbered steps, icon + heading + short text
:END:

*** 1. Discovery Session

We learn about your background, goals, budget, and timeline. No obligation — just an honest conversation.

*** 2. Pathway Recommendation

We identify institutions and programs that match your goals — including career outcomes and migration potential.

*** 3. Application & Enrollment

We prepare and lodge your application, negotiate offers, and secure your Confirmation of Enrollment (CoE).

*** 4. Visa & Pre-Departure

We handle your student visa application and prepare you for arrival — accommodation, insurance, orientation.

*** 5. Ongoing Support

Once you're studying, we're still here. Academic guidance, welfare support, and career planning throughout.
