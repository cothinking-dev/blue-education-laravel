** Academic Requirements
:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: Feature list — heading + text
:LAYOUT: Vertical list, clean
:END:

Every education level has its own entry point. Here's what you'll need — and remember, if you don't meet one requirement directly, there's almost always an alternative pathway.

*** Foundation Programs

Completed Year 11 (or equivalent) in your home country. Foundation programs are specifically designed as a bridge to university — they exist because the system wants you to succeed.

*** VET/TAFE

Requirements vary by qualification level and program. Some accept Year 10 completion; others require Year 12. These programs are practical, career-focused, and many can pathway directly into a Bachelor degree.

*** Bachelor Degrees

Australian Year 12 equivalent, OR a completed VET Diploma or Advanced Diploma. If your school system doesn't align perfectly with Australia's, a Foundation program can bridge the gap.

*** Postgraduate Programs

A relevant Bachelor degree or equivalent. Some programs also ask for work experience — which can actually work in your favour if you're a mature student looking for a career change.

*** Professional Programs

Programs in teaching, nursing, and engineering may have additional registration requirements. Creative arts may require a portfolio or interview. We'll guide you through the specifics for your chosen field.

*** Here's the truth about "not qualifying"

Most students who come to us worried about not qualifying end up in Australia within a year. The system is designed with pathways — not dead ends.

1. *ELICOS* — Intensive English courses (10–30 weeks). The most common pathway. Focused, effective, and many institutions offer packaged enrolment.
2. *Foundation Studies* — Pre-university programs that prepare you academically and culturally. Think of it as a head start, not a detour.
3. *VET Diploma* — Vocational qualifications that can pathway into a Bachelor degree. You earn a qualification along the way.
4. *Packaged Offers* — English + academic program combined with a single visa application. One process, one visa, clear progression.

We've helped students from 40+ countries find the right pathway. Whatever your starting point, we'll map the route forward.
