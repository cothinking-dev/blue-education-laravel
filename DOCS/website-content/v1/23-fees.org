We understand the weight of this decision. You're not just paying for a service — you're investing in your future, your family's future, maybe a whole new life. That's why we refuse to be vague about money.

Since 1998, our founding principle has been "Honesty is the Best Policy." That applies to everything — including costs. Here's what that looks like in practice:

- *Full transparency* — every fee is communicated clearly during your initial consultation, before you commit to anything
- *No hidden costs* — if there's a charge, you'll know about it upfront
- *Realistic expectations* — we'd rather give you honest numbers than comfortable ones that surprise you later

Our fees vary because your situation is unique. The complexity of your visa history, the services you need, the pathway that makes sense for you — all of this shapes the cost. That's why we start with a free consultation: so we can understand your situation before quoting a number.

*Your initial consultation is always free.* Come with your questions. Leave with clarity.

** What You'll Pay
:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: Accordion — expandable sections
:LAYOUT: Clean accordion, icons
:END:

*** Blue Education Service Fees

Our fees cover the full journey: consultation, pathway planning, application processing, document compilation, and ongoing support throughout your time in Australia. Because every case is different, we provide personalised quotes rather than generic price lists. You'll know exactly what you're paying for and why.

*** Student Travel Advice & Consent (STAC)

STAC charges depend on your visa history and personal circumstances. We calculate these transparently and explain every component so there are no surprises. Our fee calculator is available as a reference tool to help you estimate costs.

*** Medical Examinations

Visa applications require health assessments. Depending on the type of examination needed for your visa subclass, costs range from *$240 to $380*. We'll tell you which examination you need and what to expect.

*** Visa Application Fees

These are set by the Australian Government Department of Home Affairs and vary by visa subclass. They're completely separate from our service fees. We'll provide you with the exact figures for your specific visa type during consultation.

*** Tuition Fees

Tuition is paid directly to your educational institution and varies by institution, program, and duration. We help you compare options and factor tuition into your overall budget planning.

** Your Responsibilities
:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: Feature list — icon + text
:LAYOUT: Vertical list, 5 items
:END:

Beyond our service fees and government charges, you'll need to budget for these ongoing costs. We'll help you estimate realistic figures during your consultation:

- *Accommodation* — homestay with a vetted family, rental, or student housing. We can arrange homestay through the Australian Homestay Network.
- *Guardianship* — required for students under 18, arranged through Professional Student Care Australia or International Student Alliance.
- *OSHC* — Overseas Student Health Cover. Mandatory for all student visa holders. Think of it as your healthcare safety net.
- *Living expenses* — food, transport, personal spending. We'll give you realistic figures for your chosen city.
- *Tuition* — course fees paid directly to your institution.
