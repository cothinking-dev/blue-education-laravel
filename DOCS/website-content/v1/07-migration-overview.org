** The Migration Pathway
:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: Timeline — horizontal, 4 stages
:LAYOUT: Connected steps with icons, left to right on desktop, vertical on mobile
:END:

*** Study

It starts with a student visa. You arrive, you study, you begin to feel what life in Australia is really like. This is your foundation.

*** Graduate

You finish your degree and earn the right to stay — 18 months to 4 years on a graduate work visa, depending on your qualification. Enough time to build real experience and real connections.

*** Work

An employer sees your value. Or your skills land you on the occupation list. Either way, your career in Australia takes shape.

*** Settle

Permanent residence. The moment "visiting Australia" becomes "living in Australia." Your family can join you. This is home now.

/Every story is different. Some people follow this path step by step. Others jump ahead or take a detour. We plan for your life, not a template./
