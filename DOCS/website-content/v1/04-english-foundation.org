*** What is ELICOS?

English Language Intensive Courses for Overseas Students — offered at private colleges and university colleges across Australia. These aren't generic language apps or textbook drills. You'll be in a classroom with other international students, learning to communicate in the real-world English you'll actually use at university, at work, and in daily life.

*** How long does it take?

That depends on where you're starting from.

| Your Starting Point | Recommended Duration |
|---------------------+----------------------|
| Beginner to lower-intermediate | 30 weeks (Extended) |
| Intermediate | 20 weeks (Comprehensive) |
| Upper-intermediate | 10 weeks (Intensive) |

Your Blue Education counselor will help you choose the right duration based on an honest assessment of your current level and your target entry requirements.

*** What are Foundation programs?

You've finished high school. But maybe the education system in your country doesn't align perfectly with Australian university entry requirements. Maybe you need to brush up on specific subjects. Foundation programs bridge that gap — they're designed specifically for international students in your situation.

*** What you walk away with

- Academic skills in your chosen field, taught the Australian way
- Subject-specific preparation that directly feeds into your degree
- Guaranteed university pathway options at partner institutions
- Credit towards your future degree — so you're not starting from scratch
- The confidence that comes from understanding how Australian academics think, write, and assess
