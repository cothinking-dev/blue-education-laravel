** Our Story
:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: Two-column — text left, image right
:LAYOUT: 60/40 split
:IMAGE: Archival team photo or Perth office
:END:

In 1998, a small group of education and migration professionals in Perth saw a problem. International students were getting generic advice from commission-driven agencies. Nobody was putting the student first.

So they started Blue Education on a simple principle: give honest, independent advice — even when it's not what the client wants to hear.

Twenty-five years later, that principle hasn't changed. We've helped thousands of students from over 40 countries navigate Australia's education system, launch careers, and build permanent lives here. We've done it one student at a time, because that's the only way to do it properly.

We're still boutique. We're still in Perth. And we still believe that honesty is the best policy.

We're still a small team in Perth. We like it that way. Because when you trust someone with your future, they should know your name.
