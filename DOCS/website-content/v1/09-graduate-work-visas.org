*** Body

The Subclass 485 visa is your bridge from study to career. It lets you live, work, and study in Australia after completing your qualification — giving you time to gain real work experience, build your professional network, and explore permanent residence pathways.

*** Two Visa Streams

*Graduate Work Stream*
- Duration: 18 months
- For graduates with skills and qualifications on relevant skilled occupation lists

*Post-Study Work Stream*
Duration varies by qualification level:

| Qualification   | Duration |
|-----------------+----------|
| Bachelor Degree | 2 years  |
| Master Degree   | 3 years  |
| Doctoral Degree | 4 years  |

:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: BentoBox — 2 cards

*** Temporary Skill Shortage (TSS) — Subclass 482

For skilled workers with a job offer from an approved Australian employer. Allows you to work in Australia for up to 4 years and can lead to permanent residence.

*** Employer Nomination Scheme (ENS)

Direct pathway to permanent residence through employer sponsorship. Your employer nominates you for a permanent position.

Both the Subclass 485 and employer-sponsored visas include family inclusion options — your partner and dependent children can be included in your application.

The Subclass 485 is your bridge between student life and your career in Australia. It gives you the legal right to live, work, and continue studying — and the time to build real experience, grow your professional network, and start thinking about permanent residence.

For many of our clients, this visa is the turning point. It is where Australia stops being "the place I studied" and becomes "the place I am building my life."

We have guided graduates through this transition since 1998 — across every qualification level, from trade certificates to doctoral degrees. We know the timelines, the requirements, and the pitfalls to avoid.

*** Graduate Work Stream

*** Post-Study Work Stream

|-----------------+------------|

:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: BentoBox — 2 cards

*** Temporary Skill Shortage (TSS) — Subclass 482

Your employer values you enough to sponsor your visa. The TSS visa lets you work in Australia for up to 4 years — and it can lead to permanent residence. We handle the application so neither you nor your employer has to navigate the complexity alone.

*** Employer Nomination Scheme (ENS)

This is the direct path to permanent residence through your employer. They nominate you for a permanent position, and we manage the nomination application, labour market testing, and compliance. For many clients, this is the moment everything clicks into place.
