** What Is a Student Visa?
:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: Two-column — text left, key facts card right
:LAYOUT: 60/40 split
:END:

*** Body

The Student Visa (Subclass 500) allows international students to study full-time at a registered Australian institution. It covers courses from primary school through to doctoral programs.

Getting it right matters. A poorly prepared application can mean delays, extra costs, or refusal. We make sure your application is complete, compliant, and compelling.

*** Key Facts
:PROPERTIES:
:LAYOUT: Card with bullet points, slight background
:END:

- *Visa subclass:* 500
- *Duration:* Length of your course + buffer
- *Work rights:* Yes (limited hours during term)
- *Family inclusion:* Eligible dependants can apply
- *Health insurance:* OSHC mandatory

** What Is a Student Visa?
:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: Two-column — text left, key facts card right
:LAYOUT: 60/40 split
:END:

*** Body

The Student Visa (Subclass 500) is your entry point to studying in Australia. It covers everything from primary school to doctoral programs at registered institutions.

But here is what no one tells you: the application itself can make or break your plans. A missing document, an unconvincing GTE statement, incomplete financial evidence — any one of these can lead to delays or refusal. And when you have already told your family, quit your job, or started planning your new life, a refusal is devastating.

That is exactly why we exist. We have prepared thousands of student visa applications over 25 years. We know what the Department of Home Affairs looks for, and we make sure your application gives them no reason to say no.

*** Key Facts
:PROPERTIES:
:LAYOUT: Card with bullet points, slight background
:END:

- *Visa subclass:* 500
- *Duration:* Matches your course length, plus a buffer period
- *Work rights:* Yes — limited hours during term, more during breaks
- *Family inclusion:* Your partner and dependent children can be included
- *Health insurance:* OSHC is mandatory (we help arrange it)

-----

** How We Help
:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: Timeline — vertical, 5 steps
:LAYOUT: Numbered steps with icons
:END:

*** 1. We Start With an Honest Conversation

Before any paperwork, we sit down and assess your situation properly. If something needs addressing — a gap in your study history, insufficient funds, or weak English scores — we tell you upfront so we can fix it before it becomes a problem.

*** 2. We Build Your Document Package

Academic transcripts, financial statements, health checks, police clearances — we give you a clear checklist tailored to your specific situation, then verify every document before it goes into your application.

*** 3. We Craft Your GTE Statement

The Genuine Temporary Entrant statement is often the most important part of your application. We help you articulate your study intentions clearly and honestly — because that is what the Department wants to see.

*** 4. We Lodge Your Application

We complete and submit everything accurately, formatted to Department of Home Affairs standards. No guesswork. No missing attachments.

*** 5. We Stay With You Until It Is Done

If the Department comes back with questions or requests for additional information, we handle it. You are never left wondering what to do next.

** Requirements Snapshot
:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: Feature list — icon + text
:LAYOUT: 2-column grid
:END:

*** Financial Capacity

You need to show you can cover tuition, living costs, and travel. This can feel daunting, but we will walk you through exactly what evidence is needed and in what format.

*** English Proficiency

IELTS, TOEFL, PTE, or Cambridge — your scores need to meet both the course requirements and the visa requirements. If you are not there yet, we can recommend preparation options.

*** Health & Character

A medical exam ($240–$380) and police clearance certificates from every country you have lived in. We guide you through the process so nothing is missed.

*** Confirmation of Enrollment

A valid CoE from a CRICOS-registered institution. If you have not enrolled yet, our education counselors can help you find the right course and secure your place.
