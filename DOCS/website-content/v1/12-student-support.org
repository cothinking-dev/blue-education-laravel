Fees vary based on individual circumstances and service requirements. Key cost considerations:

- *Medical examinations*: $240–$380 depending on assessment type
- *Accommodation, guardianship, and OSHC*: Student responsibility; fees vary by arrangement

All fees are communicated transparently during initial consultation. No hidden charges.

** What It Costs
:PROPERTIES:
:SECTION: Info card
:LAYOUT: Clean, warm background
:END:

We believe in honesty about costs — it's one of our founding principles. Fees vary based on your child's specific situation and needs, but here's what to expect:

Medical examinations for visa purposes range from $240 to $380. Accommodation, guardianship, and health cover fees depend on the arrangement and duration. Additional services like document translation, airport transfers, and 24/7 emergency hotline access carry separate fees.

Every cost is communicated clearly during your initial consultation. We'll give you a realistic picture — not a sales pitch.
