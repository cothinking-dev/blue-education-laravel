The Australian School System

Australia's school system spans 13 years:

Stage Years Ages Focus
PreparatoryFoundation~5Early learning, social development
Primary1–6 (or 1–7)6–12Core subjects across all learning areas
Lower Secondary7–10 (or 8–10)12–16Broader learning with growing elective choices
Upper Secondary11–1216–18Subjects chosen for further education or career
  • • Compulsory until age 16 in most states and territories
  • • Public and private schools available — we match your child to the right fit
  • • Blue Education is an SCSA Associate — officially named by SCSA to promote the WA curriculum internationally. Learn about SCSA →

§4 · What We Handle — Once They're There: 2×2 card grid (parallel services)

Once They're There

These services run in parallel — not a sequence

[icon]

Guardianship

Legal guardianship arranged through Professional Student Care Australia or International Student Alliance. Required by the Department of Home Affairs for unaccompanied students under 18.

Full guardianship details →
[icon]

Welfare Monitoring

Regular welfare checks, school liaison, and parental reporting — so you always know how your child is settling in, not just at enrolment time.

Full welfare support details →
[icon]

Cultural Adaptation

The first weeks in a new country can be disorienting for young students. We provide ongoing integration support. For a structured introduction, our Buddy Programme places students alongside Australian peers for 14 days.

Learn about the Buddy Programme →
[icon]

School Transfer & Change of Programme

Already enrolled but need to change schools or programmes? We manage the full transfer — notifying the institution, updating enrolment records, and advising on any visa implications.

§5 · For Parents — 60/40 two-column: text left, image right

For Parents

Sending your child overseas is a significant decision. Here's what we put in place before they board the plane:

  • ✓ 24/7 emergency hotline — for parents and students, any time
  • ✓ Regular welfare reports — school updates and wellbeing checks
  • ✓ Direct communication with guardianship providers
  • ✓ On-ground support in Perth and across major Australian cities

Your child is never on their own. Neither are you.
