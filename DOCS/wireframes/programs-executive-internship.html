§2 · What It Is — Two-column, text left, key facts right

What It Is

The EIP places business graduates and final-year students with Australian employers for structured, mentored workplace experience. Not entry-level administration — a real position with agreed objectives, a dedicated supervisor, and a formal performance review at the end.

The programme is built around our six-phase Employability Booster Programme (EBP), which starts before placement and continues through to review. Blue Education manages the matching, preparation, and employer coordination. You bring the commitment.

§3 · The Six-Phase EBP Process — Vertical timeline, numbered

The Six-Phase EBP Process

The Employability Booster Programme starts before placement and runs through to formal review.

1

Phase 1 — Assessment

Initial review of your background, strengths, and readiness for Australian employment. You complete an Initial Placement Questionnaire and an online readiness module before the programme begins.

2

Phase 2 — Readiness

CV preparation, LinkedIn presence, presentation guidance, and context on Australian workplace culture — what employers expect and how to meet those expectations.

3

Phase 3 — Profiling

We research target organisations, develop approach templates, and match your profile with employers suited to your background and goals.

4

Phase 4 — Preparation

We build a shortlist of target employers with you, prioritise them by fit, and prepare you for interviews and workplace expectations before any approach is made.

5

Phase 5 — Placement

Blue Education facilitates introductions, assists with interview preparation, and coordinates your internship placement once an employer confirms.

6

Phase 6 — Review

Structured performance review at the end of placement. Feedback from your employer, a final debrief with the programme team, and documented outcomes for your records.

§6 · For Host Employers — Two-column feature cards

For Host Employers

What's Involved

Hosting an EIP intern means providing a supervisor, agreed learning objectives, and a genuine working environment. Blue Education handles the matching, objectives framework, and performance review structure. There is no obligation to hire after placement.

  • —Commitment: 16 hours per week for up to 8 weeks
  • —Dedicated mentor or supervisor
  • —Orientation and agreed task expectations

What You Gain

  • —Access to motivated, pre-assessed business graduates
  • —An early look at talent before any hiring decision is made
  • —Fresh perspectives and skills from candidates with international education backgrounds
  • —No recruitment cost or hiring obligation
