§3 · Education FAQs — Accordion (details/summary)

Education

How do I choose the right course?

We assess your background, goals, and budget and recommend matching programs from 1,100+ institutions and 20,000+ programs.

What English level do I need?

Accepted tests: IELTS, TOEFL, Cambridge CAE, Pearson PTE. Scores valid for 2 years.

Minimum IELTS scores by program level
Program Level IELTS Minimum
Certificate I–IV5.5
VET/TAFE5.5 – 6.0
Bachelor6.0 – 6.5 (no band below 6.0)
Bachelor Honours6.5 (no band below 6.0)
Graduate Cert/Diploma6.5 – 7.0
Masters6.5 – 7.5 (no band below 6.5)
Doctoral6.5 – 8.0 (no band below 7.0)

Not at the required level? ELICOS courses (10–30 weeks) can bridge the gap. English & Foundation →

Can I change courses after I start?

Usually yes, but it may affect your visa conditions. Talk to us before making any changes.

§4 · Migration & Visas FAQs — Accordion

Migration & Visas

How long does a student visa take?

4–8 weeks typical. We review applications for completeness before submission to avoid delays.

Can I work while studying?

Yes. Student visa holders can work up to 48 hours per fortnight during term and unlimited hours during breaks.

What happens after I graduate?

You may be eligible for a Graduate/Post-Study Work Visa (Subclass 485). Two streams:

Graduate Work Stream: 18 months — for graduates with skills on the skilled occupation list.

Post-Study Work Stream:

Post-Study Work Stream visa duration by qualification
Qualification Duration
Bachelor2 years
Master3 years
Doctoral4 years

Both streams provide full work rights and a pathway to permanent residency. Graduate & Work Visas →

§5 · Career FAQs — Accordion

Career Services

Do you guarantee job placement?

No. We provide career counselling, job readiness training, employer introductions, and internship placements. These significantly improve employment prospects but do not guarantee placement.

When should I start career planning?

Before choosing your course. Career goals should drive education decisions, not the other way around.

Student Support

What if there is an emergency?

Contact Blue Education directly for emergency support. All Blue Education clients are covered.

Do you provide accommodation?

Yes. Through the Australian Homestay Network — vetted families, meals included. Guardianship arranged for under-18 students through an approved guardianship provider. Student support services →
