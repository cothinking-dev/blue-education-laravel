14-Day Immersion High school students

Buddy Programme

High school students join Australian classrooms, live with vetted homestay families, and explore Western Australia through structured field trips — all within a supervised 14-day programme run in partnership with Anglican Schools.

Official SCSA Partnership International schools & students

SCSA Associate

Blue Education is appointed by SCSA — the WA Government's curriculum authority — to help international schools implement the Western Australian curriculum, including the WACE programme, internationally.

Premium Programme Business & commerce graduates

Executive Internship

Strategic work experience for business graduates. The Executive Internship Programme (EIP) runs twice a year with limited places. A six-phase structured programme with real employer placements and executive mentorship.
