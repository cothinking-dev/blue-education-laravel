§2 · The Career Pathway — Horizontal timeline, 6 stages

The Career Pathway

Six structured stages — from assessing your starting point to advancing your career in Australia.

1

Assess & Plan

One-on-one session to map your skills against your goals. We identify gaps, outline realistic pathways, and review your resume before you start applying.

2

Prepare

Interview techniques, professional networking, LinkedIn optimisation — plus the unwritten expectations that affect how you're perceived from day one.

3

Build Experience

Industry-specific internships and work placements give you the Australian work history that makes your resume credible to local employers.

4

Connect with Employers

Direct introductions to employers we work with — not job boards, not cold applications. Recruitment support guided by advisers who know your field.

5

Secure Your Visa

Your career plan and your visa must align. We assess your work visa eligibility, prepare your application, and coordinate employer sponsorship where required.

6

Advance Your Career

The Executive Internship Programme offers senior-level placements with executive mentorship for business graduates and experienced professionals. Limited places, twice a year.

§3 · Executive Internship Programme — Highlight card, premium styling
Premium Programme

The Executive Internship Programme
