§2 · How We Help — 55/45 two-column: text left, image right

How We Help

Choosing where and what to study in Australia isn't just an education decision — it's a career decision, a migration decision, and a life decision.

Your adviser maps out institutions and programmes against two questions: what career do you want, and what does your visa pathway look like? The course recommendation comes after that — not before.

Once you've decided, we take care of the rest — application, enrolment, and student visa included.

§4 · The Placement Process — vertical timeline, 5 numbered steps

The Placement Process

1

Discovery Session

We assess your background, goals, and eligibility. One conversation to understand where you're starting from and where you want to end up.

2

Pathway Recommendation

You receive a shortlist of institutions and programmes matched to your career and migration goals — with a clear explanation of why each one fits.

3

Application & Enrolment

We prepare your documents, lodge applications, and secure your offer and enrolment confirmation.

4

Visa & Pre-Departure

Student visa, accommodation, health insurance, arrival orientation. All coordinated in one place.

5

Ongoing Support

Academic guidance, welfare monitoring, and career planning for as long as you're studying.

Think of entry requirements as a starting point, not a ceiling. There's almost always more than one way in.

VET post-study work rights (Graduate Work Stream, Subclass 485, 18 months) are available to graduates whose qualification and occupation appear on a skilled occupation list. Eligibility depends on course and occupation — your adviser will confirm during assessment.
